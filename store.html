About Our Store

At Mithila Chitrakala, we bring the beautiful art of Mithila to life. Our store offers Mithila paintings, handmade decorations, designer fabrics, and cultural crafts. Each item is carefully made, combining traditional skills with modern style to create something special. Whether you want a unique painting or a culturally inspired decoration, Mithila Chitrakala is the perfect place to find it.

Along with art, we also have products for a modern lifestyle, including gaming accessories, fashion items, stylish accessories, home decorations, headphones, mousepads, and more. Our goal is to keep tradition alive while embracing new ideas, making sure every product has a story—whether it’s about culture, creativity, or modern style.
